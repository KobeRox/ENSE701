About Us

TreeCo is a company which imports, grows and sells trees as well as gardening products related to growing and maintaining trees. PlantATree is a web application that is accessible by anyone through the convenience of their computer or phone. They can use this app to buy trees online from a national chain of 10 nursies.


TreeCo companys main market segments are:

Landscape gardeners – doing work for others. My main market is these people. The tool should support landscape gardeners. Housing Developers – for new subdivisions public spaces. High volume, specific tree types. Very profitable but sporadic. App not aimed at this group specifically. Local Councils – for public places. Medium volume. Very profitable and return business, but sporadic. App not aimed at this group so much. General public (do-it-yourself gardeners) – currently one-off or low volume. This is the market segment I would like to grow. Risk of public making a poor choice that we get blamed for – I want to lower that risk.
